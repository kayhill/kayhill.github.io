Our Services

Single Family Residential (1004)

This is the most commonly used report, known within the industry as the URAR, and is completed on FNMA form 1004. This appraisal report requires a full interior & exterior inspection of the subject property.

Income Properties

Non-owner occupied property valuations note area rents, location, age, condition, room count, design, and appeal. This appraisal provides a property's estimated or actual rent through analaysis of comparable properties.

Condominiums (1073)

Condominium appraisals completed for conventional and FHA loans are reported on the Individual Condominium Unit Appraisal Report (Form 1073) and comply with USPAP, and any client specific guidelines.

Vacant Land Valuations

An appraisal done on a vacant piece of land or to determine the value of an improved piece of land.
